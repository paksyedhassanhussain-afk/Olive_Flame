About Olive Flame

Our Story

The Beginning

From a Small
Hearth to a
City Icon

In 2008, Marco Varela left his position at a renowned Parisian kitchen with a single conviction: that Mediterranean fire-cooking deserved a home of its own. With a cast-iron hearth, three tables, and a relentless pursuit of perfection, Olive Flame was born.

Today, sixteen years and three Michelin stars later, that original flame still burns at the heart of everything we do. We have never lost sight of what matters most — the guest experience, the ingredient, and the moment.

🏛️
"A restaurant is not a business. It is a relationship between the cook and the guest — and that relationship must be sacred."
Marco Varela, Founder
MV
👨‍🍳
The Culinary Mind
Marco Varela
Executive Chef & Founder

Born in Valencia to a family of fishermen and farmers, Marco Varela developed his palate long before he ever held a knife. Trained under three Michelin-starred mentors across France and Italy, he spent a decade mastering classical technique before returning to his roots in open-fire Mediterranean cooking.

His philosophy is simple: let the fire speak, let the ingredient sing, and the chef's job is merely to listen and arrange.
